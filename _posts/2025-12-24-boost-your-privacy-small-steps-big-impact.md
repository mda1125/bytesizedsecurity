You don’t need to be a tech expert to protect your privacy.

Start with just one step today: Delete an old account you don’t use anymore. Less data = less risk.

Next step? Disable location tracking on apps that don’t need it.

Privacy isn’t one big leap — it’s a bunch of small steps that add up.
