Our Approach to Cookies

At ByteSizedSecurity, we believe in transparency and minimal data collection. We do not use cookies to track you, analyze your behavior, or serve targeted advertisements.

This site uses only one essential cookie, which is required for security purposes and is not set by us directly.

Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration Type
__cf_bm Cloudflare Bot management and security protection. This cookie helps distinguish between humans and automated bots to protect our content from abuse. 1 hour Necessary

What We Don't Do

  • No tracking cookies — We don't use Google Analytics or similar tracking tools
  • No advertising cookies — We don't serve ads or use retargeting
  • No social media cookies — We don't embed trackers from social platforms
  • No profiling — We don't build profiles of our visitors

Third-Party Services

Our site uses a few external services that may set their own cookies:

  • Cloudflare — Provides CDN and security services (sets the __cf_bm cookie listed above)
  • SendFox — Handles our newsletter signup (only if you subscribe)

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that blocking the necessary security cookie may affect your ability to access certain content.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all third-party cookies

Questions?

If you have any questions about our cookie policy, feel free to contact us.
