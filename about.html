About ByteSizedSecurity

Cybersecurity insights in digestible portions.

ByteSizedSecurity is an independent cybersecurity blog dedicated to breaking down complex security topics into understandable, actionable insights.

Our Mission

In an era of increasingly sophisticated cyber threats, staying informed is your first line of defense. We aim to bridge the gap between technical security research and practical understanding, making cybersecurity accessible to everyone from beginners to seasoned professionals.

What We Cover

  • Vulnerability Analysis — Deep dives into the latest CVEs and how they impact your systems
  • Penetration Testing — Tools, techniques, and methodologies for ethical hacking
  • Malware Research — Reverse engineering and analysis of malicious software
  • Web Security — XSS, CSRF, SQL injection, and modern web attack vectors
  • Cryptography — Understanding encryption, hashing, and secure communications
  • Blue Team Operations — Defense strategies, incident response, and threat hunting
