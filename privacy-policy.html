Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ 'now' | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}

Introduction

ByteSizedSecurity ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We keep data collection to an absolute minimum. Here is what we may collect:

  • Newsletter subscription data — If you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address through our provider, SendFox.
  • Contact form submissions — If you reach out to us via the contact page, we collect the information you provide (name, email, message).
  • Server logs — Our hosting provider and CDN (Cloudflare) may automatically collect standard log data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used for security and performance purposes only.

What We Don't Collect

  • No analytics tracking — We do not use Google Analytics or any similar tracking services
  • No advertising data — We do not collect data for advertising or retargeting purposes
  • No social media tracking — We do not embed social media trackers on our site
  • No profiling — We do not build behavioral profiles of our visitors

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Deliver newsletter content you have subscribed to
  • Respond to your inquiries or messages
  • Maintain the security and performance of our website
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — Provides CDN, DNS, and security services. Cloudflare may process certain data as described in their privacy policy.
  • SendFox — Handles newsletter distribution. If you subscribe, your email is stored with SendFox per their privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages — Hosts our website. See GitHub's privacy statement.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for security. For full details, please see our Cookie Policy.

Data Retention

Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form messages are retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry. Server logs are managed and rotated by our hosting and CDN providers per their respective policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email
  • Object to processing of your personal data

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
